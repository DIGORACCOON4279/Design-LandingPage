Roller Seed FAQs

– Everything You Need to Know! -

What is Roller Seed?

Roller Seed is a design-focused app that helps you explore, create, and organize your design inspirations efficiently. Whether you’re a professional designer or a creative enthusiast, Roller Seed provides intuitive tools to enhance your workflow.

Is Roller Seed free to use?

Yes! Roller Seed offers a free version with essential features. For advanced tools and premium content, you can upgrade to our Pro plan.

What platforms is Roller Seed available on?

Roller Seed is available for both Android and iOS. You can download it from Google Play and the App Store.

Can I sync my designs across multiple devices?

Absolutely! With a Roller Seed account, you can sync your projects and preferences across all your devices for a seamless experience

How can I get support if I encounter an issue?

You can visit our Help Center within the app or contact our support team at support@rollerseed.com. We’re here to assist you! Let me know if you want any modifications or additional FAQs! 🚀
